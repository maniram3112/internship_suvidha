About Conference

The 2nd IEEE International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Quantum Computation Based Sensor Applications - 2024 will be held on 20th-21st December 2024 at the G H Raisoni College of Engineering and Management, Nagpur, Maharashtra, India.

This conference aims to unite the global community of researchers, academics, and practitioners to explore and discuss the latest advancements and future prospects in the rapidly evolving fields of AI and quantum-based sensor applications. Accepted papers will be considered for submission in IEEE Xplore, Scopus Indexed.
